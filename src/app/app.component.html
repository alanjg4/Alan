Income > 50k Predictor

Loaded the UCI Adult dataset and split into train/test. Cleaned and combined numeric and one‑hot encoded features to ensure consistent indexing and string column names. Executed GridSearchCV over key parameters (max_depth, min_samples_split, n_estimators) using accuracy as the scoring metric, selected the optimal hyperparameter set, and resulted in a reduced train/test accuracy gap compared to the default RandomForestClassifier model. Trained a XGBoost classifier on the processed training data, then independently ran a GridSearchCV‑wrapped XGBoost on the same split. Recorded 0.887% training vs 0.872% test accuracy for the XGBoost model and 0.877% vs. 0.886% for the grid‑searched model to directly compare their performance.
